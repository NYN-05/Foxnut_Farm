## üéØ General Guidelines for All Icons

### **Design Principles**:
1. **Simplicity**: Keep designs minimal and recognizable at small sizes
2. **Color Harmony**: Use the Foxnuts Farm color palette consistently
3. **Flat Design**: Modern flat 2D style, no 3D effects or heavy shadows
4. **Clarity**: Icons should be instantly recognizable
5. **Consistency**: Similar stroke widths, corner radius, and style across all icons

### **Technical Specifications**:
- **Format**: SVG, JPG, or PNG (High Quality)
- **Size**: Square format, minimum 512√ó512px (1:1 aspect ratio)
- **Background**: Fully filled - NO transparent areas or borders
- **Coverage**: Icon/image must fill the ENTIRE square space edge-to-edge
- **File Size**: Optimized but maintain quality (under 200KB per image)
- **Display**: Used with `object-cover` CSS - will fill card completely
- **Compatibility**: Ensure cross-browser support

### **Color Usage**:
- **Primary Color**: Use the specified color from the palette as dominant color
- **Full Coverage**: Fill the entire square with color and design - no empty space
- **Accents**: Use complementary colors from the palette for details and depth
- **Contrast**: Ensure sufficient contrast for white text overlay at bottom
- **Background**: No transparent areas - fully filled design

### **Styling Effects**:
- **Full Coverage**: Design must extend to all edges - no padding or borders
- **Gradients**: Can use gradients to add depth and visual interest
- **Layering**: Can use overlapping elements to create rich, full designs
- **Text Readability**: Design should work with white text overlaid at bottom
- **Edge-to-Edge**: No circular boundaries - fill the complete square space
